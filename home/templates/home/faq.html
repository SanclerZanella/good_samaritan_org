Frequently Asked Questions

The below questions are our most frequently asked questions at The Good Samaritan Org. If your question hasn't been answered below, please get in touch through the contact us page.

Who receives food parcels?

Our shelves are always in need of food for our individuals and families who are facing poverty and health problems.

Often we hear that people who come to us for food are worried whether they can feed themselves or their families or if they will be forced to choose between food and other life essentials.

How do food parcels provide a hand up?

For someone to receive food assistance for a prolonged period is a great relief.

Food is at the base of the life essentials, well nourished our community can keep health treatments, studies and hope of a prosperous future.

How do I change details on my account?

To change any details on your account, please log in to your account using the email address and password you chose when you created an account. In your account dashboard, use the form to change your details.

How do I set up a recurring donation?

You can choose one of the three options of sponsorship (sponsor a kid, sponsor a widow or sponsor an Elderly), and your card will be charged every month with the chose sponsorship value. Just one sponsorship is allowed per user or email.
